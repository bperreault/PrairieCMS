Worship ministry

The purpose of Shalom' worship ministry is to provide an opportunity for people to worship God. The team does this by teaching and inspiring worshippers through the following:

Singing, Fellowshipping, Praying, Giving of time, talents and treasures

We hope that worshippers are convicted by the Holy Spirit to believe in God and to accept His calling on their lives and obey His will. People devote their time, talents and treasures in the following areas of ministry.

Sunday Morning Worship Team

This team is composed of musicians, vocalists, and technicians dedicated to creating an atmosphere for people to worship together. There is also opportunity for people to share various gifts and talents which draw worshippers closer to God. Rehearsal times are normally Thursdays from 6:30 to 8:30 p.m. Beginning mid-May through August, practices are Tuesday evenings. 

Youth Worship Team

Shalom youth (Middle School through High School) meet for worship on Wednesday nights, which is led by many of the youth alongside the Worship Pastor and other adult volunteers. This ministry is a vibrant part of Shalom' youth.

If you would like to get involved in the Worship Ministry at Shalom you can complete the Worship Ministry Application and return it to the church or email it to Pastor Adam.
