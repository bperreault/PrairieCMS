 Student Ministries

 We love our teens and see them as the next generation of leaders. We Desire to meet them each on their own ground and walk alongside them giving them the  love, motivation and wisdom to become the men and women God desires. 

 Shalom Student Ministries: Wednesday Night Youth Group

 6:30-8:00 p.m. Middle School

 7:30-8:00 p.m. Combined Youth Worship

 7:30-9:00 p.m. High School

 Wednesday Night Youth Group has a heavy emphesis on worship and discipleship but with a deeper focus on allowing as many of the youth (who are ready) lead  this program. We want this to be the place where the youth get to use their gifts and talents to serve the Lord and have it be a fun and safe place for them to bring  their friends.

 Youth Sunday School

 9 a.m. High Schoolers meet in the Heritage Room; Middle Schoolers meet in the Youth Room.
